About Us

We are changing millions of lives through technology and Charity.

Founded in 2019, Nairocare is a social enterprise that applies technology and innovative tools to raise funds and resources for Internally Displaced Persons(IDPs) in Africa. A pioneer in the field of social advocacy married with the use of financial technology tools. Nairocare uses an array of technology tools to simplify fundraising and crowdfunding for the Internally Displaced Persons(IDP) across Nigeria.
